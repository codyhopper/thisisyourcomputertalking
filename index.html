VoiceOver is controlled by your keyboard. The shortcut for turning it on is the same as turning it off: Cmd + F5.

You'll also need the “Voiceover key” which is the Ctrl and Option keys pressed and held at the same time. From here on out, these will be referred to as the VO keys.

Continue⤳

After VoiceOver is launched, you'll be able to navigate laterally across elements on the screen with the VO key and the ⇠ and ⇢ arrow keys.

Continue⤳

After navigating to the web content, you can enter the content by pressing VO + Shift + ⇣.

Continue⤳

Here are those steps again:

  1. Launch VoiceOver Cmd + F5.
  2. Navigate to web content VO + ⇠/⇢ .
  3. Enter web content VO + Shift + ⇣ .

When you are ready, use the ☀ keys and turn your screen brightness all the way down. Then, launch Voiceover to interact with what only the screenreader can see on this website.
